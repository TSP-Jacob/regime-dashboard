Regime Definitions

Goldilocks Growth

Economic Characteristics: Moderate GDP growth (2-4%), controlled inflation (PCE near 2% target), healthy labor market, both ISM Manufacturing and Services above 50, Fed policy neutral to slightly accommodative.

Market Environment: Risk-on sentiment, equity valuations expand, credit spreads tight, volatility (VIX) typically below 20.

Trading Approach: Bullish bias. Full position sizing. Aggressive dip buying, take breakouts, ride trends. High conviction trades with wider targets (2-3R). Win rate 55-60%.

Inflation Scare

Economic Characteristics: Strong growth (GDP >3%) but inflation accelerating (PCE >3%), Fed hiking or threatening to hike, real rates rising or turning positive, ISM still strong but input prices elevated.

Market Environment: Risk-off rotation, growth stocks underperform, bond yields rising, yield curve may flatten or invert, volatility elevated.

Trading Approach: Bearish bias. Reduced sizing (60-75%). Fade rallies, short reversals, selective dip buying. Tighter stops (0.75-1.0 ATR), quicker profit taking (1.5-2R). Intraday focus.

Recession / Crisis

Economic Characteristics: Negative or very weak GDP growth, both ISM Manufacturing and Services below 50, unemployment rising (Sahm Rule triggered), Fed cutting aggressively, credit spreads widening.

Market Environment: Severe risk-off, equities trending down, flight to quality (bonds, USD), VIX elevated (>30), correlation breakdown.

Trading Approach: Aggressive bearish bias. Full size on shorts. Short every bounce, fade breakouts, avoid dip buying. Wide stops on shorts (1.5-2.0 ATR), let winners run (3-5R).

Recovery / Reflation

Economic Characteristics: GDP accelerating from low base, ISM rising above 50 (expansion), unemployment stable or falling, inflation still low, Fed very accommodative (low rates, QE possible).

Market Environment: Strong risk-on, cyclicals outperform, commodities rally, yield curve steepening, "reflation trade" in full effect.

Trading Approach: Aggressive bullish bias. Full+ size (can oversize). Very aggressive dip buying, take all breakouts. Wide stops (1.5-2.0 ATR), trail runners (3-5R). High conviction.

Stagflation

Economic Characteristics: Weak growth (GDP <2% or negative) combined with elevated inflation (PCE>3%), ISM weak but input prices high, Fed in impossible position (can't hike or cut), unemployment rising.

Market Environment: Extremely challenging - no safe haven. Equities struggle, bonds offer no refuge (yields rising), commodities may be only positive performer. High volatility, low correlation.

Trading Approach: Neutral/defensive. Minimal sizing (30-50%). Mean reversion only, fade all breakouts, range trade. Very tight stops (0.5 ATR), scalp mentality (1R targets). Preserve capital.
